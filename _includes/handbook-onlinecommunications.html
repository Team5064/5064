Outside of meetings the team communicates using our Facebook group web page. All students and mentors are required to have an active Facebook account, to be members of the FRC 5064 Facebook group, www.facebook.com/groups/opcomrobotics/ and to actively and regularly check the group page. This form of communication is by far the fastest and most efficient mode of communication.
