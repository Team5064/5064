About Opcom Robotics

Opcom's mission is to show students of all ages that science, technology and problem solving are not only fun and rewarding, but help develop communication, leadership and teamwork skills.

FIRST Lego League (FLL)

The FIRST LEGO League (FLL) introduces young people to the fun and excitement of science and technology while building self-confidence, knowledge and life skills. Alongside adult mentors students between the ages of 9-14 solve problems with engineering concepts and presentation techniques using LEGO Mindstorms. More information about Opcom's FLL program.

FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC)

The FIRST Robotics Compeition (FRC) combines the excitement of sport with science and technology to create dynamic, challenging environment for learning. FRC helps high-school students discover rewarding and engaging world of innovation and engineering. More information about Opcom's FRC program.
