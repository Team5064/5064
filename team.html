We are Team 5064, a FIRST Robotics Competition team based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. Our goal is to actively and continuously strive to improve ourselves and others by practising and promoting the Core Values of FIRST both in and out of competition..., oh, and we build awesome robots too. Our team's motto is Go FAST., where FAST stands for

  • Fast: as in the physical quantity velocity having both a speed and direction
  • Agile: as in quickly, smartly and resourcefully responding to change
  • Strong: as in having great physical and mental strength and resilience
  • Trained: as in mastering the necessary skills to excel and reach our goals

By going FAST we continuously challenging ourselves, our team and our robot with the goal of reaching our full potential. We do not settle for easy solutions, we strive for FAST solutions.
